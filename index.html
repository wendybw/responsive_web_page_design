Edinburgh




Edinburgh, Scotland's capital, is a city that beautifully blends history and modern life. Set against the dramatic backdrop of Edinburgh Castle
and surrounded by picturesque hills, it's a place where the past meets the present.The city is famous for its old and new parts, both recognized
as UNESCO World Heritage Sites.[1] Its charming streets, historic landmarks, and a lively arts scene make it a top destination for tourists and scholars.
